Three ways organizations use carbon

Every carbon project may look similar on the ground, but organizations participate in carbon markets for very different reasons. Understanding that objective explains why they make different commercialization and financing decisions.

Commercialization

Commercialization is the decision about how much future carbon value to monetize today, and how much upside to retain for later.

Choose a commercialization strategy

Monetize todayRetain future upside

Financing

Financing is not just debt. It is the question of how much future carbon value can support today's costs before credits are verified and sold.

Advance buyer support

Useful when a buyer wants secure future supply and is willing to share delivery risk.

Investor capital

Useful when a developer wants to retain upside but needs capital to build the project pipeline.

Blended funding

Useful when carbon supports a broader mission and cannot carry all early-stage risk alone.
